About The Program

Bridging the Gap Between Campus and Corporate

Arshith Fresh India Pvt. Ltd. and Suntech Solutions have partnered to create an immersive environment for undergraduate students (Degree & 3rd Year B.Tech). We don't just teach theory; we build professionals.

Throughout this 6-month journey, you will be treated as an entry-level developer, given real responsibilities, actual client requirements, and guided by senior tech leads who have decades of industry experience.

Live Projects

Work on applications that will actually be deployed and used.

Corporate Exposure

Learn SDLC, Agile methodologies, and daily stand-up routines.

The Journey

6-Month Transformation Roadmap

A highly structured curriculum designed to take you from fundamentals to full-stack mastery.

Month 1: The Foundation

UI/UX & Web Basics
  • Figma & Canva Design Principles
  • Project Documentation (DPR, Costing)
  • HTML5, Advanced CSS3, Responsiveness

  • Cloud Hosting basics (AWS, PythonAnywhere)

  • End-to-end development of a Live Project

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to apply?

This program is specifically designed for undergraduate students, with a primary focus on Degree & 3rd Year B.Tech students who want to build a career in software development.

Does this internship guarantee a job?

While it does not guarantee a job, only students who successfully complete this 6-month internship will be eligible to participate in the exclusive campus recruitment drives conducted by Arshith Fresh and Suntech Solutions.

What happens if I miss a session?

We treat this as a professional environment. While occasional academic leaves are permitted with prior notice, consistent attendance is required to earn the final certification and placement eligibility.
